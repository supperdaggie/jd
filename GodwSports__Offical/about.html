Mission Statement

At God Within Sports, our mission is to have a deeper understanding of faith in Jesus Christ through the lens of professional athletes, reaching individuals across the world with the transformative message of the good news. We are committed to empowering athletes to share their faith and connect with others who share their values. Our platform provides a safe and supportive environment for athletes to build meaningful relationships and grow in their faith journey.

Our Team Values

At God Within Sports, we are guided by our core values: faith, empowerment, love, and fellowship. Our faith in Jesus Christ drives us to share the transformative message of the good news through the lens of professional athletes. We empower athletes to use their platform to share their faith and values, connecting them with others who share their beliefs. We provide a safe and supportive environment for athletes to express their faith and build meaningful relationships, fostering a community that uplifts and supports each other in their faith journey.

Our Team Vision

Our vision is to create a global community where individuals are inspired by professional athletes sharing the good news of Jesus Christ, leading to personal transformation and a deeper connection with their faith. We envision a platform that not only empowers athletes to share their faith but also engages listeners worldwide, fostering a community of believers who are uplifted, encouraged, and transformed by the message of hope and love in Jesus Christ.

About Us

At God Within Sports, our mission is to have a deeper understanding of faith in Jesus Christ through the lens of professional athletes, reaching individuals across the world with the transformative message of the good news. We are committed to empowering athletes to share their faith and connect with others who share their values. Our platform provides a safe and supportive environment for athletes to build meaningful relationships and grow in their
faith journey.
